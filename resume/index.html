Hello, My name is Jenna Godwin, and I love to dream big, smile alot, get down to work, hack electronics and code. I am currently in my 2A term of Computer Engineering at the University of Waterloo, and I am loving it!

My experience is mainly regarding coding and software, however I am strongly interested in obtaining more real world experience with hardware and electronics. In the mean time I have been working with Raspberry Pi and Arduino SCB's.

In my spare time, I love to run outdoors with my husky puppy, read a good historical fiction, take apart electronics and see what makes them tick, I participate in cultrual dancing, like to learn languages, and eat exotic foods.
